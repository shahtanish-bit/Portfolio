Hackathon
February 28, 2026

Campfire Event

Biratnagar, Koshi Province, Nepal

The hackathon was a game jam in which 260+ college students participated as teams, and then there was me, a 14-year-old teenager, solo. The theme “beneath the surface”. The theme required you to build a game in which the player was either playing/moving under the surface, or its goal was to go above the surface in the game using game mechanics, or anything related to the theme “beneath the surface”. This was my first hackathon or any tech-related event, so I tried to socialize as much as I could to increase my tech social circle. I started to work on my project as soon as the event started, and keeping the theme in mind, I made a game named “Glitch World.”

The game was based on the concept of not seeing glitches as bugs but as features. It starts as a regular puzzle game, you spawn in a 2d world where you are a cube and have to navigate towards a portal which will take you above the surface, but you are trapped inside a boundary as a cube, and the portal is also enclosed in a boundary. So, you have to use glitches(which are also game mechanics) like phase(wall hack), gravity flip(flips gravity), and many others to complete 20 levels. I made this game to stand out, I used this concept, and it worked by helping me achieve 14th rank among 260 participants.
